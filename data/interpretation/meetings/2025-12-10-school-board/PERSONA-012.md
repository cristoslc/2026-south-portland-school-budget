### Reactions

Okay so basically they spent three hours talking about hiring someone to help them hire someone, and somehow managed to not mention students a single time. Like, we're literally the people who have to live with whoever they pick as superintendent, but apparently our opinions don't matter at all.

The whole thing was kind of wild honestly. Three different companies came in trying to get hired, and the prices went from like $8,000 to potentially $35,000. And these board members who are always talking about budget cuts and how broke we are? They picked the most expensive one. The interim superintendent literally said "we have zero" money for this in the budget, and they just voted to spend thirty grand anyway.

What really got me was when board member Richardson said the meeting room was empty and nobody from the community showed up. Like, maybe that's because you never actually tell students these meetings are happening? Or because you schedule them at 5 PM when people are getting dinner and doing homework? But then they act all surprised that nobody cares. The whole thing just felt like adults making decisions about our lives without us, which honestly is pretty much how everything works around here.
