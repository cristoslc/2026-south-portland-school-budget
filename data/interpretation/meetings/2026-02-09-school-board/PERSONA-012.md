Okay so I went to this school board meeting and honestly it was a lot. Like, the good news is that Angela gave this amazing report about all the stuff happening at SPHS - theater's still going strong, we have this new "Little Kitchen" thing where students are organizing food donations for families during the ICE stuff, and winter sports are doing well. Like, that's not "we might have to reduce some programs" - that's "your classes might not exist." And the elementary thing is wild - they want to completely reorganize all the elementary schools by grade level instead of having neighborhood schools. The board chair was like "I think this is fabulous, kids are more flexible than parents think" but honestly that sounds like something adults say when they're about to make a decision that affects kids without actually asking us.

The one thing that was actually awesome though was that they voted to let Angela and our other student rep Alex actually interview the people who want to be our next superintendent. Like they have to do confidentiality training and everything, but they'll be in the room asking questions and giving input. That's never happened before and it feels like they're finally taking student voices seriously on something that actually matters. I just wish they were asking for our input on the budget stuff too - like, we're the ones who are gonna have to live with whatever they decide about class sizes and which teachers get cut.
