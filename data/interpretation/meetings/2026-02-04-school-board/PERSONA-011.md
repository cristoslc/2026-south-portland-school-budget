And health insurance costs are looking at 12% increase instead of the 8% they thought earlier. The whole thing feels like they keep finding more bad news the deeper they dig.

The one piece of good news is they're NOT talking about closing any schools, which honestly I was worried about. They say they need the space for pre-K expansion that the state is requiring.
