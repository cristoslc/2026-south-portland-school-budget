Oh my god, I watched this whole 2+ hour meeting online after my toddler went to bed and I'm honestly not sure why I put myself through it. How am I supposed to participate when meetings go until 9 PM and I can't get a babysitter? The only thing that actually made me happy was hearing about the tree lighting tomorrow night. FINALLY something designed for families with little kids! But seriously, if this is what city government is like - endless discussions about committee budgets and regional transportation boards - I'm starting to understand why more parents don't pay attention. None of this felt like it was about the actual South Portland my daughter is going to grow up in.
