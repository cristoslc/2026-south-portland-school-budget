# FY27 Budget Development

When developing our budget, we keep the following filters in mind: 

- Maintain and continue to improve critical programs.

- Ensure that we are best positioned to live out our mission in 2026-27.

- Develop a budget that ensures fairness for taxpayers and effectiveness for the 
district.

We appreciate the communities desire to gather information as part of the budget development process.  In order to use our resources wisely we are collecting all requests here.  We will review requests, and publish information gathered with all stakeholders.
