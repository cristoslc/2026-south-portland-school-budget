# Cumulative Record: Maria (PERSONA-001)
## Meeting: School Board Workshop -- December 10, 2025

### Interpretation

This meeting crystallizes everything Maria has come to distrust about the school board's priorities and decision-making process. After months of watching the district spiral into crisis - from the initial vague warnings about a "difficult budget year" to the shocking revelation of a $1.9M current-year deficit requiring immediate spending freezes on classroom supplies - the board just voted to spend $30,000 on a superintendent search consultant while admitting they have "zero" budgeted for it.

The hypocrisy is staggering. Maria has watched teachers lose access to basic classroom materials, seen 78 positions slated for elimination including 42 teachers, and witnessed her child's elementary school potentially facing closure through reconfiguration. She's coordinated PTA support to fill gaps the district can't fund, watched parents offer comprehensive community resources only to be dismissed with vague promises, and seen donations become essential lifelines rather than nice extras. Now the same board that imposed spending freezes and cut positions finds money for the most expensive search firm option.

What infuriates Maria most is the board's justification: they chose Zeal because it keeps decision-making with the board rather than involving community screening committees. After all her advocacy for parent input, after months of feeling shut out of budget decisions that directly impact her children, the board explicitly chose a process that minimizes community involvement. Rosemary's admission that "no one came" to hear these presentations reveals how disconnected they are from the families they serve.

The timing makes it worse. This search is happening while the budget crisis unfolds in real time. Maria knows that by the time a new superintendent arrives, the damage will be done - teachers laid off, programs cut, schools potentially closed. The board is spending premium dollars on leadership recruitment while the system they're recruiting for crumbles around them.
