Questions › Schools & Facilities
Will my child's school close or be reorganized?
Parents Teachers
Short answer
Likely yes. The board is evaluating 9 reconfiguration options that would reorganize five K-4 schools into grade-band campuses (two pre-K–1, three grades 2–4). A recommendation is expected at the March 23 workshop.
What we know

The district faces a $7.2M structural deficit, and elementary reconfiguration is projected to save $1.5–2.2M — making it the single largest savings lever under consideration. [1]

At the March 2 workshop, 24 parents spoke against reconfiguration and 51 data requests were filed, but the board voted unanimously to advance the proposal. [2] ⚑ flag
