## Key Observations

**Total enrollment decline:** 3,096 (2013-14) to 2,816 (2024-25), a decline of 280 students (-9.0%).

**Elementary (K-4) decline:** Kindergarten from 259 to 200 (-23%); total K-4 from 1,244 (2013-14) to 1,030 (2024-25), -17%.

**Pre-K growth:** 28 (2013-14) to 51 (2024-25), +82% — reflects CDS pre-K expansion.

**High school (9-12) relative stability:** 850 (2013-14) to 928 (2024-25), actually increased +9.2%.

**Middle school (5-8):** 974 (2013-14) to 807 (2024-25), -17%.
