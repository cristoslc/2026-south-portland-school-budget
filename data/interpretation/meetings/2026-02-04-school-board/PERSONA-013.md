Mom, this budget meeting was actually really scary. They're talking about completely changing how elementary schools work - instead of kids going to school in their neighborhood, they'd put all the little kids in one building and older elementary kids in another building. That means like a 2nd grader and their 4th grade sister might not even go to the same school anymore, which is so messed up. And they want to make classes bigger too - from like 16 kids to 22 or 23 kids in each class. You know how I already feel like I can't get help sometimes when there's too many kids? It's gonna be even worse.

They kept talking about "looking under every line in the budget" which basically means they're going to cut everything they can find. I'm worried they're going to cut my gifted program or make band only part-time like they've been talking about. It's not fair that they hired more teachers when there were fewer kids, and now kids like me have to pay for their mistakes by having our schools all mixed up and our classes overcrowded.

I don't understand why they can't just figure out how to spend money better instead of making our school experience worse. Like, they're talking about traffic studies for the middle school but they can reorganize all the elementary schools? It doesn't make sense and nobody asked us kids what we think even though we're the ones who have to live with whatever they decide.
