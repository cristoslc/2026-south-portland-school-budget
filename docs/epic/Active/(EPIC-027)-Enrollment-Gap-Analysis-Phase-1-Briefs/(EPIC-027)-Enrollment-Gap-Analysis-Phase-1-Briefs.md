# Enrollment Gap Analysis & Phase 1 Briefs

## Goal / Objective

Audit the district's published enrollment basis against what a responsible school closure decision requires. For each gap, document what question should have been answered, what evidence exists, what's missing, and why it matters for this specific decision. Produce per-persona briefs that surface these gaps with interventionist framing — "here's what you need to know before this decision gets made, and here's what nobody has shown you yet."

## Desired Outcomes

Residents understand the enrollment assumptions underlying the closure decision and can evaluate whether those assumptions are supported by publicly available evidence. Each persona receives this message in their own frame: Maria hears it as "they're deciding your kid's future without this data," Tom hears it as fiscal exposure, Linda hears it as governance risk. The district faces pressure to either release its internal projections or justify the decision without them.

## Scope Boundaries

**In scope:**
- Systematic audit of district enrollment claims against EPIC-026 data
- Gap identification with severity assessment (critical / important / informational)
- Per-persona Phase 1 brief generation through interpretation pipeline
- Publication to site via [INITIATIVE-004](../../../initiative/Active/(INITIATIVE-004)-Public-Budget-Site/(INITIATIVE-004)-Public-Budget-Site.md)

**Out of scope:**
- Independent projection (that's EPIC-028)
- Transportation implications (that's INITIATIVE-006)
