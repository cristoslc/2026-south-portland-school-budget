The union's been trying to get people to understand that you can't just "find efficiencies" when you're talking about this kind of structural deficit. When Rosemary talked about all those former students she's stayed connected with - that's exactly what we're going to lose if we gut the teaching force. Those relationships, that continuity, the ability to really know our kids. But nobody wanted to talk about that tonight.
