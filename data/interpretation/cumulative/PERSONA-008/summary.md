# Cumulative Summary: Rachel (PERSONA-008)

As of March 19, 2026, Rachel understands the South Portland school budget crisis not as a discrete fiscal problem but as the convergence of chronic institutional mismanagement, deliberate process opacity, and compounding tax burdens on the same households. Nine formal elementary reconfiguration scenarios were presented at the March 2nd budget workshop, including closures of specific named schools and grade-band splits that would force every elementary student to change buildings at second grade. The full decision timeline is now public: April 7 public hearing, April 14 city council school budget workshop, May 5 council vote, June 9 referendum. Rachel has shifted her organizing target from the March school board workshops to April 14th as the new critical battleground.
