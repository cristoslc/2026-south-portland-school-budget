Oh my god, where do I even start? I knew things were bad with the school finances, but this audit was just confirmation of everything I've been saying in our parent Facebook groups. The council needs to actually oversee this department instead of just approving whatever they ask for.

At least there's the community forum tomorrow night - I'm definitely going, and I'm bringing my questions about exactly how they're going to fix this financial nightmare.
