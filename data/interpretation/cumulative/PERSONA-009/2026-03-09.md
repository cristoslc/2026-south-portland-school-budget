### Interpretation

Staff departures during a budget crisis where 50-78 positions face elimination could signal that the most mobile employees are leaving before they are pushed. Dana files this as a trend to watch: if resignations accelerate in the coming weeks, it becomes a "brain drain" story about the district losing talent before it even finalizes cuts.

This compressed timeline means the board must finalize its budget approach within three weeks. Dana is now planning crew deployment for March 23 at Redbank Community Center, which she expects to be the highest-drama session given that it will be the first workshop after the Skillin closure was killed and the board must converge on an alternative.

The Community School model approval is a positive human-interest sidebar but lacks the conflict and stakes that drive viewership. Dana notes it as potential B-roll context for a future "what the district is doing right" segment if the editorial balance ever calls for it.
