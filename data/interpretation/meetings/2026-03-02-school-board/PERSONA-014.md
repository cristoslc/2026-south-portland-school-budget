### Reactions

So basically they want to maybe close my school and make me go somewhere else next year. I don't get why they have to do this. Like, what's wrong with Dyer? I like my teachers and I know where the good bathroom is and I have friends there. 

My mom tried to explain that it's about money but I still don't understand why that means I have to leave. The grown-ups kept talking about "diversity" and "equity" which I guess means mixing kids up from different places, but I already have friends who speak different languages and look different than me. Why do we have to go to different schools for that?

The worst part is they said some kids might have to go to one school for kindergarten and first grade and then move AGAIN to another school for second, third, and fourth grade. That's so dumb. By the time you learn where everything is and get to know your teachers, you have to leave again. And what if my friends don't go to the same new school as me? 

Some of the parents were really mad and some were crying. One parent said her kid almost started crying when she told him about maybe changing schools. I would cry too. I don't want to start over somewhere new where I don't know anybody and I have to figure out where the lunch room is and which bathroom is the clean one. That's scary.

But some of the school board people said they don't want to close certain schools, so maybe mine will be okay? I just wish the grown-ups would figure this out without making us kids worry so much about it.
