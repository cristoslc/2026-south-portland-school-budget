This is what happens when you have a school board that treats taxpayer money like it's unlimited. I guarantee this mess will end up on my tax bill somehow, and they'll act shocked when voters push back on their next budget increase. The writing's on the wall - either they make serious cuts now or they'll come asking for a massive tax hike to bail themselves out.
