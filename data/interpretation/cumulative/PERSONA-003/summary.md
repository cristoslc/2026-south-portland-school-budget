Library early childhood programming spaces were removed from the Mahoney project with no alternative plan. ICE enforcement has destabilized community cohesion and school attendance, and four of seven city councilors declined to protect displaced families in February. City governance continues to operate on a track disconnected from the district's crisis. Jess is no longer planning to leave South Portland, but cannot determine which building her daughter will enter, which teachers she will have, or whether the system will stabilize before 2028.
