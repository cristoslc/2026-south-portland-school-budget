Okay, so the big headline tonight is the school department is a complete mess financially - again. Six different finance directors in five years, and they had to move $400,000 from grants back to the general fund because stuff was either ineligible or overspent. Joe Prince says the new finance director is "amazing" but honestly we've heard that before.

The good news is the city's finances are actually in decent shape - auto excise taxes came in $1.8 million over budget and the fund balance went up $5.8 million. Staff wants to expand it to include the mall area for transit-oriented development. Council seemed generally supportive but Councilor West had concerns about combining multiple TIF districts. The immigration resolution passed unanimously with an amendment asking the governor for a 60-day eviction moratorium - there was really emotional testimony from community members about families afraid to turn on lights in their homes.
