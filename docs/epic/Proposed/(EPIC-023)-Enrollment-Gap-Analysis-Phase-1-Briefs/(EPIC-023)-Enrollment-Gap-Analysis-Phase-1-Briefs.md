# Enrollment Gap Analysis & Phase 1 Briefs

## Goal / Objective

Audit the district's published enrollment basis against what a responsible permanent closure decision requires. Expose the gaps — not neutrally, but with interventionist framing that makes clear what the public deserves to know before this decision is final. Deliver per-persona briefs through the interpretation pipeline on a timeline that matters (before City Council budget adoption).

## Desired Outcomes

Maria understands what enrollment trends mean for her kids' school — not just this year's numbers but what nobody has shown her about the trajectory. Tom can evaluate whether the closure savings math holds up when you examine the enrollment assumptions underneath it. Linda has specific, sourced gaps she can raise in governance proceedings — ammunition, not talking points. Priya can see whether the enrollment analysis even accounts for the populations she advocates for.

## Scope Boundaries

**In scope:**
- Systematic audit of every enrollment claim in district closure recommendation documents
- Assessment framework: supported (evidence confirms) / unsupported (evidence contradicts) / unpublished (no evidence either way)
- Gap documentation: what question should have been answered, what evidence exists, what's missing, why it matters for the decision
- Per-persona Phase 1 briefs via INITIATIVE-003 interpretation pipeline
- Interventionist framing — "here's what you need to know before this decision gets made"

**Out of scope:**
- Independent projections (that's EPIC-024)
- Recommendations for or against closure
- Data collection (that's EPIC-022)
