## SPSD Goals 25-26 Detail

1. **Attendance** — 3% increase in number of students with attendance rate of 90% or above
2. **Belonging** — 5% increase in the percentage of students who report a sense of belonging at school
3. **Achievement**
   a. 72% of students will be at or above the 41st percentile on the NWEA Math test, with at least 3% improvement at each school.
   b. 68% of students will be at or above the 41st percentile on the NWEA Reading test, with at least 3% improvement at each school.
4. **Equity**
   a. Close the achievement gap of identified subgroups by 5% in Math & Reading.

## Clarity and Role Definition

- We hear the feedback from stakeholders about staffing in administration and director roles
- We hear the value of instructional roles
- We needed to evaluate administrative systems and structures to more clearly define the work of directors
- We want to ensure that we are utilizing these positions, and ultimately funds, efficiently and in the service of students
- Director roles help reduce risk, and elimination of these positions could have negative consequences (legal fees, out of district costs for examples)

## Percussion EdTech

- All positions that are planned for reduction in the FY27 budget play an important role.
- This position remains on the list to be reduced in the Superintendent's budget. It has not been returned.
- In FY26 the Board voted to reinstate the EdTech with the anticipation of using reserve funds. There was insufficient funding for all of FY26 and none for FY27. *(Click Here for Video of this Discussion.)*

---

## Seeking Additional Funding

- We understand the reduction of 8.4 million in district costs means significant impact.
- We know stakeholders have explored short term local and state funding measures for 'bridge' extensions in timelines
- As we consider our FY27 funding, we must continue to be thoughtful about a long term plan for financial sustainability — additions to FY27 mean a more challenging FY28 and beyond
- A sustainable path to rebuilding fund balance is essential

---

## Recommended Policy Considerations

1. Adopt a fund balance minimum threshold and usage policy
2. Adopt a budget timeline and strategy by November 1st of each year

By way of further explanation and guidance to the Superintendent with respect to the filing of the required school closing report, the Board contemplates [brief explanation re: how students will be shifted and to where — explanation for what will occur upon closure of one elementary school].
