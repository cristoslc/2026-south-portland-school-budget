# Fiscal Exposure & Family Logistics Briefs

## Goal / Objective

Produce per-persona transportation briefs that present the V1 configuration comparison findings in each stakeholder's frame. The briefs follow the metric priority established during brainstorming: fiscal exposure leads (what this costs), family logistics is the human face (what this means for mornings), governance accountability is the framing (this analysis didn't exist before the vote).

The tone is rigorous and factual. Let the numbers speak. All deliverables disclose known limitations and explicitly welcome district data that would improve the estimates.

## Desired Outcomes

Maria understands how many mornings she'd spend doing split-building drop-offs under each option. David has a dollar figure for the transportation costs missing from the budget. Rachel can compare what Option A means vs. Variant C. Linda has the implementation analysis the board should have had. Tom sees the unfunded mandates. Dana has the contradiction she can report on. Each persona gets the same underlying analysis, presented in the frame that matters to them.
